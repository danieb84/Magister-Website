Magister Teaching Assistant

Magister is a mobile application designed for the teacher on go. It makes creating a lesson plan easy while you're in transit, don't have access to a computer, or for any other convenience.This app is also a scheduler which saves the dates and class times for lessons, as you plan, in our built-in calendar. With no need for access to internet connection, you can use the app anywhere! Designed by a teacher, for teachers.

Magister is a mobile application designed to make creating a lesson plan easy while you're in transit, don't have access to a computer, or for any other convenience. It is also a scheduler which saves the dates and class times for lessons, as you plan, in our built-in calendar.

Explore Magister

use the arrows to go back & forth

Create a Schedule/Lesson Plan

You have the choose of creating a schedule or schedule & full lesson plan by selecting an option and filling out this form.

Lesson Plan

The information entered in schedule will appear on the lesson plan form. The default template for the lesson plan includes all the core items of a lesson: objectives, pre-requisites, materials, learning activity, culminating activity, and evaluation.

Stored Lessons

All lesson schedules and plans are saved on the device and can be accessed in this list. You can search for a lesson by entering the topic. Select a lesson to see a detailed view.

Detailed View

In the detailed view, the schedule and/or lesson plan is presented with options to edit, duplicate, delete, or export your lesson.

Calendar

Our built-in calendar also provide access to saved lesson. Select a date to view all the lessons scheduled for that day(listed at the bottom). Select a lesson from the list to see a detailed view.

Help Page

Our help page provides links to manuals with instructions for all tasks completed in the app.
